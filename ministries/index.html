Our Ministries

A place to grow, serve & thrive.

At SWIC we believe everyone has a place to grow, serve, and thrive spiritually.

The Beautiful Feet Choir

Leads the church in praise, worship, and song ministrations during services and special gatherings.

The Amazing Airport Stars

Welcomes members and visitors, assists with offerings, and helps maintain a clean, organized environment.

The Dancing Stars

Ministers through dance and creative expressions during services and programs.

The Seeing & Hearing Media Team

Handles sound, photography, videography, publicity, and all media operations.

The Film Stars

Shares messages and ministers through drama, acting, and creative performances.

The Protocol Team

Assists with order, security, and protocol duties, especially around the pastor and church events.

Precious Pearls

A ministry dedicated to raising young women to grow in Christ and become everything God has called them to be.
